# Single Audit — Reporting

Sampling unit: report submission.

## Pre-flight check

Before pulling, **confirm the applicable reports for the program**. Two acceptable sources:

- A **compliance supplement** for the program is provided in the conversation / uploads, OR
- The applicable reports can be located online (web-search the program / CFDA / agency for required reporting forms — FFR / SF-425, performance reports, program-specific filings)

If neither is achievable, surface to the user before pulling — sampling reports without knowing which reports are required is not workable.
