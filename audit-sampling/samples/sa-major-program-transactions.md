# Single Audit — Major Program Transactions

Sampling unit: expenditure (transaction) within a major program.

Sizing and selection follow the full single-audit workflow at `references/single-audit-workflow.md`. That workflow handles program materiality (5% of program expenditures), cross-program control minimums for payroll / disbursements (≥ 25 occurrences each), per-program substantive sizing (25 or 40 based on RMM and controls reliance), and IDC handling (≥ 1 per material program, max 2).

## Notes
- Mandatory on single audits — never skip
- Compliance Supplement defines applicable compliance requirements per program — read the relevant sections before defining attributes for each program
