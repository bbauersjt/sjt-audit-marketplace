# EBP — Disclosure and Technical Review (ASC 962 / 960 / 965)

1. Apply the correct GAAP framework based on plan type:
   - Defined Contribution (401(k), 403(b), profit sharing, ESOP): ASC 962
   - Defined Benefit: ASC 960
   - Health & Welfare: ASC 965
   - All plans: ASC 820 (fair value), ASC 825 (financial instruments), ASC 855 (subsequent events), ASC 275 (risks and uncertainties)

## Defined Contribution Plans (ASC 962)

Confirm the following disclosures:

1. Plan description — type, eligibility, employee contributions (pre-tax, Roth, after-tax), employer contributions and match formula, vesting schedule, participant accounts, investment options, payment of benefits, forfeitures, plan termination, party-in-interest references
2. Significant accounting policies — basis of accounting (accrual), use of estimates, investment valuation, income recognition, payment of benefits (recorded when paid, not when payable), administrative expenses (paid by plan vs. sponsor)
3. Investments at fair value — ASC 820 leveling (Level 1 / 2 / 3) with valuation techniques disclosed; investments measured at NAV using the practical expedient are excluded from the leveling table and disclosed separately with unfunded commitments, redemption frequency, and notice period
4. Fully benefit-responsive investment contracts (FBRICs) — measured at contract value with required ASC 962-325 disclosures including average yield and crediting interest rate
5. Notes receivable from participants — at unpaid principal plus accrued interest, NOT at fair value; disclose interest rate range, repayment terms, default policy
6. Tax status — determination letter date OR reliance on prototype/volume submitter, statement on UBTI, ASC 740 uncertain tax positions evaluation
7. Risks and uncertainties (ASC 275) — investment concentration, market risk, credit risk, interest rate risk
8. Party-in-interest / related party transactions
9. Plan termination provisions
10. Subsequent events, with date through which evaluated
11. Reconciliation to Form 5500, if differences exist (typically accrued contributions, benefits payable accrual)
12. For ESOPs: allocation method, dividend treatment, repurchase obligation, leveraged ESOP debt if applicable

## Defined Benefit Plans (ASC 960)

1. Confirm all applicable items above, plus:
   - Statement of Accumulated Plan Benefits (or equivalent presentation)
   - Actuarial present value of accumulated plan benefits (vested and nonvested) with comparative
   - Changes in actuarial present value of accumulated plan benefits (rollforward showing benefits accumulated, increase from interest, benefits paid, plan amendments, changes in actuarial assumptions)
   - Actuarial assumptions: discount rate, mortality table, retirement age — disclosed and consistent with actuary's report
   - Funded status, if presented
   - PBGC coverage disclosure, if applicable

## Health & Welfare Plans (ASC 965)

1. Confirm all applicable items from the ASC 962 framework, plus:
   - Benefit obligations including claims incurred but not reported (IBNR) liability
   - Postretirement benefit obligations, if applicable
   - Premium stabilization reserves, if applicable
   - Method for estimating IBNR

## Section 103(a)(3)(C) Disclosure (All Applicable Plans)

1. Confirm the note disclosing the Section 103(a)(3)(C) election is present.
2. Confirm identification of the certifying institution.
3. Confirm a statement that the certified information was used without further audit procedures.
4. Confirm the disclosure language is consistent with SAS 136.

## Common Disclosure Defects to Flag

1. Participant loans classified as investments instead of notes receivable
2. NAV practical expedient investments included in the fair value leveling table
3. Missing redemption frequency / notice period for NAV investments
4. FBRICs measured at fair value instead of contract value
5. Missing average yield / crediting interest rate for FBRICs
6. Stale determination letter language (referencing letters now outdated; plan should reference current restatement cycle)
7. Subsequent events evaluation date earlier than report date
8. Risks and uncertainties disclosure absent or boilerplate
9. Party-in-interest disclosure missing recordkeeper, trustee, or sponsor where applicable
10. Plan termination clause language absent
