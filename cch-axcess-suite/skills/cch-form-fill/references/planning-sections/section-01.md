# Section 01 — Pre-Engagement & Acceptance

**Forms:** KBA-200 (Entity Info), KBA-201 (Acceptance & Continuance), COR-203 (Predecessor, new-engagement only), AID-201 (Nonattest Independence). AUD-100 tailoring is handled by the risk-assessment process.

**Purpose of this file:** the complete, deduplicated list of the facts needed to answer every question in this section accurately. The forms' fillable boxes reduce to the ~19 facts below. (COR-203 stays empty until the new-engagement driver activates it.)

---

## What you need to know to answer Section 01

### Entity identity & structure
1. **Legal name; primary location; full contact block** (address, phone, fax, email, website).
2. **Entity type & legal form** — government subtype (tribe / municipality / district / etc.); the reporting entity, and any **component units** included or excluded (and the basis — e.g., excluded clinic handled via departmental presentation).
3. **Reporting framework / basis** — GASB GAAP, regulatory basis, etc.; any **GAAP departures / emphasis-of-matter**.
4. **Financial-statement date / FYE.**
5. **Number of employees.**
6. **Nature of operations** — programs, **funds presented** (e.g., General + Grant), funding sources, and **whether federal awards exist** (the Single-Audit trigger).
7. **Governance** — is there an **audit committee or equivalent**? Who is the **governing body / those charged with governance** (name it — *engagement constant*, e.g., "General Assembly").
8. **Multiple components / sites / segments** included in the FS? (Y/N + brief.)

### Engagement characteristics (drive the KBA-201 toggles)
9. **Applicable standards** — GAAP FS audit; **GAGAS / Yellow Book**; **Single Audit** (Uniform Guidance)?
10. **Integrated audit?** · **Key Audit Matters reporting?** · **Concurring/EQ review required?** — from engagement type + firm policy. *(Government FS audit defaults: integrated No, KAM No, GAGAS Yes, concurring per policy.)*
11. **New engagement vs. continuing** — drives COR-203 and opening-balance procedures. *(New to CCH is not the same as new client; a Caseware-to-Axcess move on a continuing client is continuing.)*

### Acceptance & continuance judgments (KBA-201 "are you aware of any…" block)
12–15. Awareness of any: **integrity concerns** regarding management/governance · **fraud / noncompliance / illegal acts** · **disagreements / unresolved issues / limitations on responses** · **independence threats**, and whether the firm has **competence, resources, and time**.
16. **Conclusion** to accept/continue.
> Near-invariant: 12–16 default to **"none identified / accept."** Each is a paired answer+explain box — the explanation stays **blank** unless the answer is Yes. Override only on a real exception.

### Nonattest / independence (AID-201)
17. **Nonattest services provided** (FS preparation is the standard) · the client's **designated individual overseeing** them and their **skill/knowledge/experience** · **safeguards** · independence conclusion.
> Near-invariant except the designated-individual name and the specific services.

### Predecessor (COR-203 — only if #11 = new)
18. **Predecessor auditor identity & contact** · **client authorization** to communicate · the four **inquiries** (integrity of management, disagreements, reason for change, fraud/illegal acts) and the **responses** · date of inquiry, person interviewed.

### People (reused everywhere)
19. **Key management names & titles** — finance lead, executive lead, anyone you communicate with. *Engagement constant; feeds KBA-303, AUD-901, governance communications, related-party, etc. — name the person, never "management."*

---

## Engagement constants surfaced here (gather once, reuse all sections)
Governing-body name (#7) · key management names/titles (#19) · FS date / report date (#4) · entity legal name & type (#1–2). These are the values later forms keep re-asking for.

## The genuinely variable inputs (everything else is default or fact-lookup)
New-vs-continuing (#11) · management names (#19) · nonattest designated individual (#17) · predecessor contact + responses (#18, if new) · any real acceptance/independence **exception** (#12–15). That is the short list to extract; the rest is entity facts or default answers.
