# Section 02 — Audit Strategy & Planning

**Forms:** AUD-101 (Overall Audit Program), KBA-101 (Overall Audit Strategy), AUD-201 (Opening Balances — new engagements only). **Risk-assessment:** KBA-301 Materiality, KBA-400 Scoping. **COR-205** here = Successor Auditor Acknowledgment Letter (a letter document — 0 KC fields).

Live counts: AUD-101 = 340 fillable (approximately 175 are sign-offs on standard program steps + 187 procedure texts), KBA-101 = 80, AUD-201 = 36 (new-engagement only). **Most of AUD-101/AUD-201 is standard procedures signed off with WP references — not questions.** The genuine inputs collapse to the engagement profile + team/timing + a few entity descriptors.

---

## What you need to know to answer Section 02

### A. Engagement profile — the master toggle cluster
Approximately 15 yes/no characteristics that **recur across AUD-100, AUD-101, KBA-101, KBA-201, AUD-201** (and gate content on each). Set by engagement type + firm policy; near-invariant per firm. Answer once → propagates everywhere.

- New vs. recurring engagement *(gates AUD-201 + predecessor work)*
- GAGAS / Yellow Book — **Yes** (government)
- Single Audit — Yes if federal awards
- Integrated audit — **No**
- Test operating effectiveness of controls — **No** (substantive); rely on prior-year control evidence — **No**
- Key Audit Matters — **No**
- Engagement quality / concurring review required — per firm policy
- Nonattest services performed — **Yes** (FS prep)
- SAS-146 / SQMS-1/2 early implemented — **No** (firm-wide; flips to KBA-902A if Yes)
- Substantive procedures at interim — engagement
- CAATs expected — engagement
- Access to audit documentation granted to outside parties — often **Yes** (federal/SA)
- Multiple opinion units — entity fact
- Multiple components / sites / segments in scope — entity fact

### B. Reliance / use-of-others (default No; Yes opens a branch/sub-form)
Component auditor · service organizations · internal audit function · auditor's specialist.

### C. FS presentation descriptors (entity facts — from the FS / Section 01 profile)
Type of presentation (general-purpose vs special-purpose government); list of basic statements audited; RSI & SI accompanying and whether opining on them; framework if not GASB; regulatory/jurisdiction reporting requirements; composition of the reporting entity (primary government + component units); reporting currency.

### D. Audit team & timing — the genuine *new* inputs (logistics)
Engagement team members & roles; dates for RMM discussion meeting, fraud brainstorming, year-end fieldwork start/end, manager review, partner review, EQ review, wrap-up meeting; timing of any interim procedures. *Scheduling/staffing — not in client documents; planned by the firm.*

**Fill defaults for the critical-dates / timetable form (it's a required check-the-box form — every row gets a response, none left blank):**
- **Timetable / critical dates** — make up reasonable dates from the engagement letter's timeline (fieldwork, review, report). Generic and internally consistent; this is filler, but it's required filler.
- **Engagement-team meeting & fraud-discussion rows** → forward-ref the team-discussion KC form by its index ("see engagement team discussion, [index]"), don't restate it here.
- **Physical inspection / observation of assets** → year-end if the entity has inventory to observe, else **N/A** (the firm does not observe fixed assets).
- Everything else → short, generic, unchallengeable. (Generic is fine, blank is not.)

### E. Independence documentation (KBA-101)
ET 1.295 nonattest-independence documentation prepared — **Yes**; GAGAS non-audit-services independence documentation prepared — **Yes**. (Firm prepares; ties to AID-201.)

### F. Opening balances (AUD-201 — only if new engagement)
Opening-balance procedures performed (prior closing → current opening, consistency of policies, predecessor coordination) — standard responses + WP refs. Conditional on A's new-vs-recurring; **recurring → form N/A.**

---

## Mostly procedures, not questions
AUD-101 (master program) and AUD-201 are roadmap/program forms — hundreds of sign-offs over standard steps (preliminary engagement activities, understanding the entity, preliminary analytics, component-unit search). Signed off with generic responses + WP references pointing at the strategy / analytics / entity-understanding workpapers. Handled like the concluding pseudo-programs, **not** as knowledge to gather.

## Constants surfaced here (→ master list)
The **Engagement Profile cluster (A)** — the single largest reused answer set across all planning forms. Plus **team & review milestones (D)**, reused on KBA-902/903 sign-offs and the fraud/RMM discussion forms (KBA-501).
