# Section 05 — Risk Assessment

**Form:** KBA-501 (Team Discussion / Fraud Brainstorming). **The risk-assessment process owns:** KBA-502 (Summary of Risk Assessments — IR/CR/RMM + approach), KBA-503 (Basis for Inherent Risk). The substantive risk content is handled by the risk-assessment process; only the team-discussion documentation is covered here.

Mostly standard discussion narrative.

---

## What you need to know to answer Section 05 (KBA-501)

- **Meeting date + attendees** — engagement team members. *(Constants from KBA-101 team & timing, Section 02-D.)*
- **Fraud risks discussed** — default = the two mandatory presumptions: **improper revenue recognition** and **management override of controls**, plus any specific risks surfaced in KBA-302/303. (Specific risk conclusions land in KBA-502.)
- **Standard discussion narrative** — application of the framework & susceptibility to misstatement, professional-skepticism reminder, evaluate-audit-evidence reminder, who was included. Generic firm language + sign-offs.

That is the whole form: two constants (date, attendees) + the default fraud-risk presumptions + boilerplate narrative. Anything elevated flows to the risk-assessment process (KBA-502).
