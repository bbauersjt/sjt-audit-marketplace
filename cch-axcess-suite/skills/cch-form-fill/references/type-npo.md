# Nonprofit (NPO) — Deltas vs. Core

Identical machinery to the other types (as EBP); deltas are entity-profile facts + type-specific gating toggles. Framework = FASB **ASC 958**.

---

## NPO-specific additions to the knowledge map

### 1. Entity profile additions
- 501(c) type; tax-exempt status; UBI exposure.
- Net-asset model: **with vs. without donor restrictions** (the core NPO classification).
- Funding mix (contributions/grants vs. program revenue); federal awards → Single Audit possible; GAGAS if grant-required.

### 2. Distinctive gating toggles (default per entity facts; switch program steps)
- **Contributions & support (AUD-805):** cash contributions/grants, **contributed services/facilities/in-kind gifts**, donated materials, special fund-raising events, **conditional promises to give**, future interests (life income/annuity), **agency transactions**, program service fee revenue, confirmation of contributions receivable, improper-revenue-recognition fraud risk.
- **Split-interest agreements (AUD-804):** charitable trusts (current/prior year), trustee/fiscal-agent role, life interest in real estate, pooled income funds / net income unitrusts, revocable agreements. *(Default No for most small NPOs.)*
- **Net assets (AUD-814):** net assets **with donor restrictions?** + releases from restriction.
- **UBI / tax (AUD-812):** deferred tax, uncertain tax positions — usually default No.

### 3. NPO-unique content
- **Functional expense allocation** (program / management & general / fund-raising) — ASC 958 requirement.
- **Endowment / UPMIFA** (if board-designated or donor endowments exist).
- Donor-restriction tracking and release.

---

## Carries over unchanged
Entire core spine (acceptance, entity info, fraud inquiry, COSO controls, team discussion, concluding, subsequent events), the engagement-profile cluster, and all collapse levers. Control cycles mirror commercial (Cash Receipts · Inventory · PPE · Other Assets · Disbursements · Payroll · Treasury · Income Tax · Reporting).

## Net
NPO = core + **net-asset (donor-restriction) model** + **contributions/in-kind/split-interest toggles** + **functional expense / endowment**. Toggles mostly default No except contributions and donor restrictions, which are the live ones.
