# Construction (CNS) — Deltas vs. Core

Construction is its own KC title, but its planning/concluding machinery and its
question set track **Commercial** (FASB ASC). Read this as *Commercial plus the
contract-accounting emphasis*; the core spine, engagement-profile cluster, and all
collapse levers carry unchanged (see `cross-type-core.md`). Framework = FASB ASC.

---

## The one thing that actually differs — AUD-8xx numbering

CNS shares the Commercial program map **through Fair Value (AUD-816)**; from AUD-817
the suffixes diverge because CNS carries an extra area (contracts) and has no separate
number for it. Resolve every program by its **area binding key + title**, never by
number (`cch-risk-assessment/scoping/area-map-by-title.md` is the map; `cch-axcess`
resolves the live form).

| Area | Commercial (ASB/HOA) | CNS |
|---|---|---|
| Fair Value (FAIRVALUE2) | 816 | 816 — **parity ends here** |
| Uncompleted/Completed Contracts (CONTRACT) | — | **817** (CNS-only) |
| VIE (VIE2) | 817 | 818 |
| Share-based payments (STOCK) | 818 | 819 |
| Commitments & contingencies (COMMIT) | 819 | 820 |
| Accounting estimates | 820 | 821 |
| Concentrations (CONCENT) | 821 | 822 |
| Business combinations (BUSINESS) | 822 | 823 |

Net: **AUD-817 = Contracts on CNS** (VIE on Commercial), and every Commercial
risk/disclosure program from VIE onward shifts **+1**.

## Construction-specific content (the signature delta)

- **CONTRACT (AUD-817) — the CNS area that Commercial does not have.** Contract
  revenue recognized over time (ASC 606 / percentage-of-completion), costs and
  estimated earnings in excess of billings (CIE / underbillings) and billings in
  excess (BIE / overbillings), retainage, change orders, and the WIP schedule.
  Estimate-heavy — cost-to-complete drives revenue, so management bias in the
  cost estimate is the real risk. Ties to the estimates program.
- Otherwise CNS = Commercial content: covenant/debt exposure, equity structure,
  income tax posture, PP&E (heavy equipment).

## Carries over unchanged
Entire core spine, engagement-profile cluster, all collapse levers, and the
Commercial control cycles (Cash Receipts · Inventory · PPE · Other Assets ·
Disbursements · Payroll · Treasury · Income Tax · Reporting/Closing).
