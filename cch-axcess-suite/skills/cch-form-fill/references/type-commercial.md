# Commercial — Deltas vs. Core

Mapped from live forms (pulled cross-title). Same finding: identical machinery; deltas = entity-profile facts + type-specific gating toggles. Framework = FASB ASC.

---

## Commercial-specific additions to the knowledge map

### 1. Entity profile additions
- Entity form: C-corp / S-corp / partnership / LLC.
- Tax posture (taxable vs. pass-through) → income-tax program relevance.
- Debt/covenant exposure; equity structure (shares, members).

### 2. Distinctive gating toggles (default per entity facts; switch program steps)
- **Revenue & AR — ASC 606 (AUD-803):** accounts/notes receivable, related-party sales, pledged/assigned receivables, related-party/employee receivables, long-term receivables, transferred receivables, improper-revenue-recognition fraud risk.
- **Inventory (AUD-804):** inventory observation, perpetual vs. periodic, **valuation (FIFO/LIFO)**, manufacturing/finished goods, interim physical count, public-warehouse/outside locations, pledged inventory.
- **Income taxes (AUD-810):** deferred tax provisions & DTA/DTL, uncertain tax positions.
- **Equity (AUD-812):** independent share registrar, **share-based compensation**, share subscriptions receivable, treasury shares, preferred shares, OCI items.
- **VIE (AUD-817):** VIEs where entity is/isn't primary beneficiary, group components, **PCC VIE accounting alternative** (private-company election), SAS-149 early-implementation.

### 3. Commercial-unique content
- ASC 606 revenue recognition (performance obligations, contract terms).
- Debt covenant compliance.
- Business combinations / share-based payments (if applicable).
- Private-company accounting alternatives (PCC: VIE, goodwill).

Commercial = core + **606 revenue / AR** + **inventory valuation** + **income tax** + **equity structure** + **VIE consolidation**. Toggles are entity-fact-driven (from TB/FS), mostly default No except where a balance exists.
