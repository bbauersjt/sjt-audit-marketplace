# Single Audit (8000) — Cross-Cutting Module

Mapped from live forms (S-form series pulled cross-title). **A module, not a type** — it bolts onto a Govt or NPO engagement with federal awards. Same machinery as the FS audit (profile cluster, memo-format understanding/controls, default-Yes checklists, program pattern), driven by federal-awards facts.

Trigger: scan the TB/SEFA for federal awards. None → skip the whole 8000 section. Any → open AUD-100S and the cascade.

---

## What you need to know to complete the SA module

### 1. Federal awards / SEFA (the data that drives everything)
From the client's SEFA: each federal program by **awarding agency + Assistance Listing Number (ALN)**, total expenditures, **clusters**, pass-through/subrecipient relationships (and pass-through entity + identifying number), loan/loan-guarantee programs, R&D, amounts to subrecipients, and the **10% de minimis indirect cost rate election** (yes/no). KBA-901S is the SEFA presentation/disclosure checklist (default Yes).

### 2. Major program determination (AID-301S — gated on SEFA)
Type A/B threshold (based on total federal expenditures), program risk assessment, **low-risk auditee** status (drives 20% vs **40% coverage**), which programs end up **major**. This is the SA scoping decision; it gates which 8100+ compliance programs exist.

### 3. Compliance requirements per major program (the requirement matrix — AID-702S; the recognition index calls it KBA-400S — same artifact, gated)
The **Matrix of Compliance Requirements** — for each major program, which of the 12 are **Direct & Material**: activities allowed/allowable costs · cash management · eligibility · equipment & real property · matching · period of performance · procurement/suspension & debarment · program income · reporting · subrecipient monitoring · special tests & provisions. This is the SA equivalent of KBA-400 scoping. (RA-adjacent — risk content; brain documents the matrix.)

### 4. SA engagement profile (AUD-100S — adds to the profile cluster)
Service organizations for federal program transactions? · compliance requirement types expected significant? · interim/other audits of major programs? · plus the shared toggles (new/recurring, EQ review, specialist, nonaudit services, SAS-146). Mostly firm-policy defaults.

### 5. Understanding the program environment (KBA-302S — memo format)
Federal program operations narrative: demand for program services, pass-through/subrecipient relationships, program participant relationships, program-management sophistication, complex laws/regulations, new legislation affecting programs. **Memo-format toggle** → one narrative, like KBA-302.

### 6. Controls over compliance (KBA-401S + AID-401S + AUD-701S)
COSO control environment **over federal compliance** (1,546 boxes, memo-collapsed) — whistleblower process, conflict-of-interest re federal awards, audit-committee charter for federal oversight. Same control-approach gate: substantive default, or rely on controls where tested. **Procurement exception:** where current-year procurement controls are relied on (AUD-811S, control rows Yes/Yes/Yes). Compliance-controls testing documented in AUD-701S + sampling (AID-701S).

### 7. Risk of noncompliance (KBA-502S/503S) — RA-adjacent
IR/CR/RMM of noncompliance per major program × compliance requirement.

### 8. Findings & reporting (KBA-103S/104S — gated aggregators; 103SE in some binders)
**Questioned costs**, noncompliance findings, IC-over-compliance deficiencies → flow to the schedule of findings & questioned costs and the compliance opinion. Default none; populate from actual findings (e.g., repeat material weaknesses → qualified compliance opinion on the affected ALN).

## Carries over from core
Profile cluster, memo-format collapse, paired answer+explain, root-question→N/A, procedure-vs-finding, aggregator auto-link, control-approach gate — all apply. The S-forms mirror their FS counterparts (KBA-302S≈302, KBA-401S≈401, KBA-901S = a checklist, AUD-701S = a program).

## Net
SA module = federal-awards facts (**SEFA → major programs → compliance-requirement matrix**) + the same machinery in compliance dress. The genuinely new inputs: the SEFA, the major-program/low-risk-auditee determination, and the per-program compliance-requirement matrix. Everything else is core defaults relabeled for compliance.

## Per-requirement compliance programs
This SA title is **matrix-driven** (AID-702S + AUD-701S + substantive sampling) rather than one AUD-8xxS per requirement. Program-specific additions (e.g., AUD-801S/811S) are layered on that matrix — same generic-response pattern as the FS programs.
